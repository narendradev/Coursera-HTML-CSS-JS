Bulletproof Coffee Recipe

Bullet coffee is a creamy, delicious drink that will fuel you with energy. Whether you’re looking for keto coffee or just something new to start your mornings, this drink is for you!

Ingredients :

  • Instant Coffee Powder
  • Butter
  • Water

Procedure :

  1. Boil water for 5 minutes
  2. Add instant Coffe Powder
  3. Add two table spoons of butter
  4. Blend all together for 2 minutes
  5. Pour in coffe mug
  6. Drink Coffee
